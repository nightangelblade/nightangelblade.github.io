Welcome to My Assassin's Creed Site!

Ever since I first played the first Assassin's Creed on the Playstation 3, the series has become one of the near yearly titles that I always aim to pick up. The idea and story behind it is one that I keep on wanting to come back to and learn more about.

Background: Assassin's Creed followed a secret organization, known as the Assassins, through different significant periods of time across the world, starting with the original group during the Crusades, whom were based off of the original Hashashin sect. Their enemy in this series is the Templars, based off of the original Knights Templar. Both groups are racing to gain possession of what are known as the Pieces of Eden, powerful artifacts from an ancient race known as the First Civilization, a highly advanced society who appeared to be like gods to mankind. This battle has continued all the way to modern day, where both Templars and Assassins make use of a machine, the Animus, to relive and control various individuals throughout history who were rumors to have seen or possessed the Pieces of Eden.
